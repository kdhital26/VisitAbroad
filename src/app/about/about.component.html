Our Services

Abroad Shipping Management, provide a complete ship management service. Our team of experienced superintendents and managers ensure you have the safest, most secure and cost effective vessels: whatever their size, type or location. We are based in London, operate worldwide and have been established for over one hundred years We provide a complete range of services, from new build, valuation, repair and maintenance to crewing, purchasing and accountancy We are a proven leader in the UK’s maritime industry and key players in all the world’s shipping markets We offer a service that is personal, flexible and bespoke: providing you with complete reassurance and maximum ROI Abroad Shipping Management are accredited to the ISO 9001:2022 Quality Management Standard and the ISO 14001:2022 Environmental Standard. In addition, we are represented on committees of Lloyd’s Register, Bureau Veritas and the UK Chamber of Shipping. LTSM are signatories to the Armed Forces Covenant and hold Cyber Essentials Certification.
